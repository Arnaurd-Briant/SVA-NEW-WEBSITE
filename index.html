Real-world Solutions

We leverage cutting-edge technology to solve complex problems and drive global progress.

My Doctor AI

My Doctor AI offers personalized and accessible health management solutions through advanced artificial intelligence, empowering users to track their health, analyze symptoms, and access tailored medical information and advice anytime, anywhere.

Military AI

Military AI provides advanced, real-time solutions for defense and security management, utilizing cutting-edge artificial intelligence to enhance tactical decision-making, monitor threats, and deliver actionable intelligence for military personnel, ensuring optimal performance and safety in any operational environment.

Robotics

FRC Ladybug leads the way in African robotics, pioneering innovation through FRC while also fostering STEM engagement in FTC and FLL, empowering the next generation of engineers and problem solvers through hands-on robotics challenges.

WIN Hackathons

WIN Hackathon is an innovative platform designed to foster tech skills and creativity by organizing hackathons that empower participants to collaborate, solve real-world problems, and sharpen their coding and problem-solving abilities in a dynamic, competitive environment.
